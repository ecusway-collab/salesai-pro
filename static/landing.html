Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a Twilio account?
Yes — you bring your own Twilio number (about $1/month). This means calls come from your number, you own the recordings, and you control the outreach. Twilio pay-as-you-go call costs are typically $0.013/minute.
Do I need an Anthropic API key?
Yes — you add your own Anthropic key in settings. Claude API costs roughly $0.01–0.05 per AI script generation. You only pay for what you use.
Can I use this for any type of business?
Absolutely. Primitive Solution works for any sales-driven business — health products, wealth coaching, supplements, services, and more. Fully customize the AI with your own product line and branding in your account settings.
How does the lead scraper work?
Enter a business type (e.g. "yoga studios") and a city. The system searches Google Maps and business directories and imports real businesses with contact details directly into your pipeline.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel anytime from your account portal — no questions asked, no cancellation fees.
