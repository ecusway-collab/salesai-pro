Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Vital Health Global ("we", "us", or "our") operates SalesAI Pro, an AI-powered sales automation platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when you register or use the platform:

  • Account information: name, email address, password (hashed), plan selection.
  • API credentials: Twilio, Anthropic, SendGrid, and other third-party keys you enter in Settings — stored encrypted and used only to operate the service on your behalf.
  • Lead data: contact names, phone numbers, email addresses, company names, and notes you import or manually enter.
  • Call and interaction data: call outcomes, AI-generated scripts, follow-up schedules.
  • Billing information: processed by Stripe. We never store card numbers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and deliver the SalesAI Pro service.
  • To send automated calls and emails on your behalf using your API credentials.
  • To send transactional emails (receipts, password resets, trial reminders).
  • To improve the platform through anonymized usage analytics.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with:

  • Service providers: Twilio (calling), SendGrid (email), Stripe (billing), Anthropic (AI), Render (hosting) — each bound by their own privacy policies.
  • Legal requirements: if required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Lead Contact Compliance

You are responsible for ensuring that contacts in your lead lists have provided appropriate consent to be contacted by phone or email. All leads can unsubscribe at any time via the unsubscribe link included in outreach. We honor all unsubscribe requests immediately.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting support. Lead data can be deleted at any time from the dashboard.

6. Security

We use industry-standard encryption (TLS in transit, bcrypt for passwords, encrypted storage for API keys). Access to production systems is restricted to authorized personnel only.

7. Cookies

We use a single session cookie for authentication. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at support@vitalhealthglobal.com to exercise these rights.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notice.

10. Contact

Questions? Email us at support@vitalhealthglobal.com.
