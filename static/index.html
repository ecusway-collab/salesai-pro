Welcome to Primitive Solution Sales AI

Follow these 5 steps to get your first AI-powered sales call running in minutes.

1
Find or Add Leads
Go to Lead Finder in the sidebar to scrape businesses from Google Maps, or click Leads → Add Lead to add one manually. Enter their name, phone number (with country code e.g. +18001234567), and email.
2
Create a Campaign
Go to Campaigns and click New Campaign. Give it a name (e.g. "Gym Owners — Energy Products"), choose your product focus and target audience. The AI will auto-generate your call script, SMS, and email templates.
3
Make Your First AI Call
Go to Leads, find your lead, and click the green button. The AI will call them using a realistic voice, introduce your products, and handle their responses automatically. You'll see the result in the lead's interaction history.
4
Automated Follow-Ups Run on Their Own
Once a lead is added, the system automatically schedules follow-ups: Day 0 call → Day 2 SMS → Day 5 email → Day 10 call → Day 22 email → Day 30 final call. You don't need to do anything — it runs 24/7.
5
Monitor Your Pipeline
Check the Dashboard daily to see how many leads are interested, how many calls went out, and your conversion rate. Use the Pipeline view to see leads organized by stage from New → Won.
Pro Tips: Always enter phone numbers with country code (+1 for USA/Trinidad). Use Call All Selected to dial multiple leads at once. Leads who reply STOP to SMS or click Unsubscribe in email are automatically removed from all future contact.
