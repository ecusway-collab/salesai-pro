Refund Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

14-Day Free Trial — No Risk. Every new account starts with a free 14-day trial. No credit card required. You can explore the full platform before paying anything.

Monthly Subscriptions

SalesAI Pro is a monthly subscription service billed in advance. Because you can test everything for free during your trial, we generally do not offer refunds for subscription payments after they have been charged.

However, we will issue a full refund for the most recent billing period if:

  • You experienced a significant technical failure caused by our platform (not third-party API outages) that prevented you from using the service.
  • You were charged after cancelling your subscription in accordance with our cancellation process.
  • You contact us within 7 days of the charge and describe the issue.

How to Request a Refund

Email support@vitalhealthglobal.com with:

  • Your account email address.
  • The date of the charge.
  • A brief description of the issue.

We respond to all refund requests within 2 business days. Approved refunds are processed back to your original payment method within 5–10 business days.

Cancellations

You can cancel your subscription at any time from the Billing section of your dashboard. Cancellation stops future charges. Your account remains active until the end of the paid period, after which it reverts to a limited free tier.

We do not charge cancellation fees.

Third-Party Costs

SalesAI Pro uses third-party APIs (Twilio for calls, SendGrid for emails, etc.) that you connect with your own credentials. Costs charged directly by those providers (e.g., per-minute Twilio charges) are not refundable by us — contact the respective provider for their refund policies.

Questions

Contact us at support@vitalhealthglobal.com — we're happy to help.
